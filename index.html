About Our Company

In 1929, Marion E. Wade, a former minor league baseball player, founded CleanFreshly as a mothproofing company based out of Chicago, Illinois. Just 23 years later, it expanded to include franchised carpet cleaning services


CleanFreshly is the leading provider of cleaning and restoration services in both the residential and commercial markets, operating through an extensive service network of more than 8,000 company-owned locations and franchise and license agreements.
